# Errata

All numerical results have been verified against reference implementations
in SciPy, SymPy and NumPy. A suite of 4,742 automated checks covers
symbolic identities, numerical accuracy and structural consistency. Errors
may still remain.

Mistakes, whether typographical, computational or logical, may be
reported by opening an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/bbjansen/evenwicht/issues) or by contacting
bobjansen@pm.me with the chapter number, section
and a description of the error.

Corrections are incorporated as they are confirmed and will appear in
subsequent editions.
